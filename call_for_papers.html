Scope and Interests

1st SME NITK Convention will provide a platform for researchers and practitioners to interact with one another and discuss the state-of-the-art developments in the field. The topics of the conference will cover all aspects of research and application in mining, including but not limited to:

  • Ventilation problems in deep open cast mines
  • Locating trapped miners underground from surface
  • Ventilation of long tunnels
  • Artificial Intelligence in Mining Industry
  • Latest developments in Rock Mechanics
  • Bulk Material Handling
  • Coal & Energy
  • Mining & Exploration
  • Mine Economics
  • Geo-Mechanics & Ground Control
  • Geological Modelling & Resource Estimation
  • Sustainable Mining
  • Health, Safety & Environment
  • Surface & Underground Hazards
  • Acid Mine Drainage
  • Mine Ventilation
  • Mine Tailings
  • Application of IT in Mining Industry (Smart Mine)
  • Advanced Mining Methods & Machinery
  • Disaster Management
